Product.

The Peel Book is a book 100% biodegradeble
To create this paper, peels go through the hydration process and are then liquefied and processed, creating a paste which is then separated into equal parts and flattened using a rolling pin so that its length and thickness is identical to a normal sheet of paper. The Peel Book is designed so that this process can be carried out twice, in order to extend the life of our 100% ecological notebook.
The UN objective that we manage is that of "Life on Earth". This consists of preserving the existence of species of flora and fauna in ecosystems. Deforestation is a major problem for the extinction of many of the animals that live here, as they lose their homes, therefore, the food chain is interrupted and does not function properly. 27,000 trees are cut down every day, just for the creation of toilet paper. This gives us an idea of the massive destruction we seek to avoid. With our notebook made from potato peels, we reduce the number of trees that are cut down to make paper, and we also use less water than these companies waste. It helps us preserve habitats, plus it's easy to make at home.
Did you know that about 17 trees are used to make one ton of paper? We have to take into account that a tree provides oxygen for 3 people to breathe per day, and because of this problem we have decided to make a paper that helps to stop deforestation. "Peel Book" is an innovation to the world of the conventional notebook because its sheets are made 100% of potato peels and it is very easy to make.
